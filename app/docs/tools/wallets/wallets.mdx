# Keplr

### Mainnet

Coreum Mainnet is whitelisted in Keplr wallet. To add Coreum to your wallet follow next
instructions:

1. Go to your Keplr wallet

    <img className="w-96 max-w-full mb-10" src="/images/tools/wallets/step1.png" alt="Keplr wallet" />

2. Click on burger menu in the top left corner and click on "Manage Chain Visibility"

    <img className="w-96 max-w-full mb-10" src="/images/tools/wallets/step2.png" alt="Keplr wallet Step 2"  />

3. After previous steps you need to see next screen:

    <img className="w-96 max-w-full mb-10" src="/images/tools/wallets/step3.png" alt="Keplr wallet Step 3" />

4. In the input type "Coreum" word to see available chains and select "Coreum" from the list as
   presented below:

    <img className="w-96 max-w-full mb-10" src="/images/tools/wallets/step4.png" alt="Keplr wallet Step 4" />

5. Click save button to add this chain to your visible chains

6. Once you finished with all previous steps, you should see "Coreum" in your chains list:

    <img className="w-96 max-w-full mb-10" src="/images/tools/wallets/step5.png" alt="Keplr wallet Step 6" />

# D'Cent

-   If you want to use Biometric Wallet, you need to update the firmware to the v2.25.0 or higher to
    create/manage Coreum account in the wallet.

    Firmware Update guide can be found here:

    -   [From PC (Mac/Windows)](https://userguide.dcentwallet.com/biometric-wallet/firmware-update-from-computer)
    -   [From Mobile using OTG Cable(Android Only)](https://userguide.dcentwallet.com/biometric-wallet/firmware-update-from-mobile)

-   [Download mobile app](https://dcentwallet.com/MobileApp)
-   [How to enable testnet](https://userguide.dcentwallet.com/mobile-app/mobile-app-setting-menu#how-to-activate-testnet)
