# Keplr

### Mainnet

Coreum Mainnet is whitelisted in Keplr wallet. To add Coreum to your wallet follow the next instructions:

1. Go to your Keplr wallet

    <img className="w-96 max-w-full mb-10" src="/images/tools-and-ecosystem/wallets/step1.png" alt="Keplr wallet" />

2. Click on the burger menu in the top left corner and click on "Manage Chain Visibility"

    <img className="w-96 max-w-full mb-10" src="/images/tools-and-ecosystem/wallets/step2.png" alt="Keplr wallet Step 2"  />

3. After previous steps you need to see the next screen:

    <img className="w-96 max-w-full mb-10" src="/images/tools-and-ecosystem/wallets/step3.png" alt="Keplr wallet Step 3" />

4. In the input type "Coreum" word to see available chains and select "Coreum" from the list as presented below:

    <img className="w-96 max-w-full mb-10" src="/images/tools-and-ecosystem/wallets/step4.png" alt="Keplr wallet Step 4" />

5. Click the save button to add this chain to your visible chains

6. Once you finished with all previous steps, you should see "Coreum" in your chains list:

    <img className="w-96 max-w-full mb-10" src="/images/tools-and-ecosystem/wallets/step5.png" alt="Keplr wallet Step 6" />
